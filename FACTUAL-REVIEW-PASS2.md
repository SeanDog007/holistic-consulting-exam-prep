## 🟡 MINOR CONCERN — Not Wrong, But Worth Noting

### Q619 — RA: "Whole grains and dairy rich foods" grouped together
**Q:** "Which of the following is not well supported for rheumatoid arthritis?"
- A: Eliminate all food allergens
- B: Whole grains and dairy rich foods ✓
- C: Vegetarian diet rich in fiber and antioxidants
- D: Eliminate added sugar and refined carbohydrates

**Concern:** Dairy elimination IS standard in holistic RA protocols (inflammatory trigger), so "dairy-rich foods" being the wrong answer makes sense. But **whole grains are generally supported** for RA (anti-inflammatory, fiber). Grouping them with dairy in one option means a well-read student might hesitate.

**Verdict:** Answer is defensible — the dairy component makes B the best answer. No change needed, but if Betsy wants to tighten it, she could change B to just "A diet rich in dairy products" without the whole grains.

**Priority:** LOW — cosmetic improvement only.
